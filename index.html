Welcome to the Global Earthquakes Dashboard



This dashboard presents earthquake data from the United States Geological Survey API . By default, earthquakes reported in the last 24 hours are displayed and are categorised by their magnitude. To view more information about an earthquake, click or tap any point on the map. You can zoom to a continent or select the number of days to display data below.
